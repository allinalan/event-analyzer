Welcome to your Event Analyzer

Upload your event tracking files year by year. Each upload adds to a permanent vault stored in this browser, so your 2024 and 2025 data stays put when you upload 2023 or 2026. The only manual step is categorizing each event in the Rank column of your spreadsheet.

Required columns (any sheet that matches will be loaded)

  • Event — event name (with or without leading ID)
  • Start Date & End Date — used to bucket by year & month
  • Rank — your category (Elite, Key, Traditional, Service Event, Mesa Marketplace, Malls, RLT, Industry)
  • Total or Total CPO — total CPO / sales
  • Total Orders or Total 2 or Orders — total orders

Multiple sheets per file are fine — the dashboard reads them all and auto-splits by year using the dates. Re-uploading a year replaces it (so updates work).

Recommended workbook format

For cleanest results, keep all events for a year on a single tab using these columns: Event, Start Date, End Date, Rank, Total, Booth, S/C, F/L, Other, Total Orders (also accepted: Total 2, Orders). The dashboard handles multi-tab files too, but a single master tab per year is fastest and least error-prone.

How event matching works

Events are matched across years by their name (the part after the leading ID like 00094424 -). The dashboard normalizes spacing, case, and punctuation so Mesa Market Place Swapmeet-January and Mesa Market Place Swapmeet - January match. Month-specific events (like Mesa Market Place Swapmeet - February) are treated as the same event year over year only when the month is the same.

About storage

Your data is stored locally in this browser only — nothing is uploaded to a server. If you open this file in a different browser or in a private/incognito window, you'll need to re-upload. To migrate your data to a different machine, copy/paste the JSON from your browser's localStorage (key: cutco_event_dashboard_v2), or just re-upload your spreadsheet.
